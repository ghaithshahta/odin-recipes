Slow Cooker Chipotle Chili

My friend Keni's love of smoky chipotle peppers inspired this chili recipe. It comes together easily in the slow cooker and is a unique chili that always stands out. It is a spicy yet mellow chili that gives a kick without the lingering burn, like many chilis do.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 pound bulk Italian sausage
  • 1 large onion, diced

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef and sausage in the hot skillet until some of the fat.
  2. Stir kidney beans, chili beans, diced tomatoes, crushed tomatoes, celery, bell peppers.
  3. Cook until vegetables are tender and celery retains a slight bite, 6 to 8 hours on Low or 3 to 4 hours on High.
